Popular Dishes

Dosa

Dosa is a thin pancake made from fermented rice and lentil batter. Served with chutney and sambar.

Idli

Idli are soft and fluffy steamed rice cakes, often enjoyed with sambar and coconut chutney.

Vada

Vada is a savory fried snack made from lentil dough, typically shaped like a doughnut.
