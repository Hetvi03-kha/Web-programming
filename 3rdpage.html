Delicious West Indian Cuisine

Explore the flavors of Maharashtra and Gujarat

Maharashtra: Misal Pav

Misal Pav is a popular Maharashtrian dish known for its spicy, tangy flavors. It's made with a mixture of sprouted lentils, spices, and served with pav (bread rolls).

Gujarat: Dhokla

Dhokla is a savory steamed cake made from fermented rice and chickpea batter. It's light, fluffy, and often enjoyed as a breakfast item or snack, accompanied by chutney.
